## 1. About us

**Scandic IT ApS** (CVR 32342396) is a Danish IT Asset Disposition (ITAD) provider based in Aalborg. We help organisations retire end-of-life IT equipment securely — through certified data erasure, device diagnostics and grading, refurbishment, physical destruction of storage media, and compliant recycling.

**Facility:** Tarmvej 8, 9220 Aalborg Øst, Denmark

## 2. Our commitment

Scandic IT operates an integrated Information Security and Quality Management System aligned with:

- **ISO/IEC 27001:2022** — Information Security Management
- **ISO 9001:2015** — Quality Management

Our management system is designed to protect customer data and assets throughout their lifecycle in our custody, and to deliver consistent service quality to every customer.

## 3. How we protect customer data

- **Secure data erasure** aligned with **NIST SP 800-88 Rev 2** and **IEEE 2883-2022**
- Per-device erasure certificates, serial-matched, retained for customer retrieval
- Physical destruction via certified shredder (ProDevice OMS900) for devices that cannot be securely erased
- **GDPR-compliant processing** — we operate as a data processor under documented Data Processing Agreements with customers as controllers
- **Chain of custody** maintained from collection to final disposition

## 4. Physical and operational security

- 24/7 CCTV surveillance across the processing facility
- Biometric access control to the depot and processing areas
- Secured perimeter with steel fencing
- Isolated network (closed VLAN) for data erasure operations, separated from general office traffic
- Visitor registration and escort procedures
- Documented chain-of-custody for every customer device

## 5. Personnel

- All staff receive security awareness training before handling customer data
- Confidentiality obligations in every employment contract
- NDA and restricted access for contractors and third parties
- Role-based access with least-privilege principles
- Formal onboarding and offboarding processes to prevent access leakage

## 6. Suppliers and sub-processors

We maintain a documented list of our sub-processors, including:

- **Montes** — Microsoft 365 managed services
- **Certus** — data erasure software
- **Piceasoft (Aiken)** — device diagnostics
- **Danløn** — payroll (internal data only)
- **DHL and partner carriers** — logistics
- **Stena Recycling, El-Recycling** — certified e-waste recycling

Our sub-processors are evaluated annually and bound by appropriate security and data-processing obligations.

## 7. International data transfers

Customer data is processed in-region by default. Where transfers beyond the EEA / UK are necessary, we rely on European Commission adequacy decisions, Standard Contractual Clauses, the UK International Data Transfer Agreement, or the EU-US Data Privacy Framework as appropriate.

## 8. Incident response and breach notification

We operate a documented Incident Response Plan covering:

- Detection, triage, and containment
- Internal escalation within defined timelines
- Customer notification in line with contractual commitments
- Regulatory notification (Datatilsynet) within statutory timelines for personal data breaches
- Post-incident review and corrective action

## 9. Governance

- Quarterly internal management review
- Annual internal audit
- Periodic external audits by an accredited certification body
- Corrective action cycle with documented follow-up

## 10. Insurance and continuity

- Building and cyber insurance through established Danish insurer
- Documented Business Continuity Plan
- Tabletop exercises to test response and recovery
