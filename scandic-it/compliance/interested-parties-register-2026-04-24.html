1 · Purpose

This register identifies the interested parties (stakeholders) relevant to the Scandic IT ApS management system, and records their needs and expectations, in accordance with:

  • ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 4.2 — Understanding the needs and expectations of interested parties
  • ISO 9001:2015 Clause 4.2 — Understanding the needs and expectations of interested parties

2 · Scope

All interested parties whose requirements are relevant to the Scandic IT ApS management system, within the scope defined in the Statement of Applicability.

4 · Legal, Statutory & Contractual Requirements

Mapped in detail under control A.5.31 "Legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements" and relevant procedures. Key instruments include:

  • GDPR (EU 2016/679) + UK GDPR — customer PII and employee data
  • Danish Bookkeeping Act + tax law — financial records
  • Danish Work Environment Act — employee safety
  • Contractual: customer cooperation agreements, DPAs, supplier contracts, carrier terms

5 · Review and Update

This register is reviewed at each management review (quarterly) and whenever:

  • A new interested party is identified (new customer type, regulator, partner)
  • An existing party's requirements change materially
  • Significant organisational change occurs
  • A new regulation comes into force
  • A corrective action or incident highlights a previously unrecognised party
