## Executive Summary

Scandic IT has operated profitably for 9 years with zero marketing. This strategy introduces structured inbound and outbound channels while preserving the operator credibility that differentiates Scandic IT from sales-driven competitors.

---

## 1. Target Customer Profiles (ICP)

### Primary ICP: Danish SMB (50-500 employees)

**Profile:**
- 50-500 employees, hybrid/remote workforce
- IT manager or Office Manager handles device lifecycle
- Pain: No structured offboarding process for devices
- Trigger: Office move, workforce reduction, device refresh cycle
- Budget: €5K-50K annually for ITAD services

**Industries:**
- Professional services (law firms, accounting, consulting)
- Tech startups (high device turnover)
- Healthcare (data sensitivity)
- Finance (compliance requirements)
- Public sector / municipalities

**Decision Maker:** IT Manager, CFO, or Office Manager  
**Influencer:** Compliance / Security team

---

### Secondary ICP: Nordic Enterprises (500-5000 employees)

**Profile:**
- Existing ITAD relationship, but seeking better coverage or quality
- Needs multi-site pickup capability
- Requires compliance documentation (GDPR, ISO)
- Often part of larger EOL tender process

**Why they choose Scandic IT:**
- Danish-based, understands local compliance
- Can integrate with Returna platform for scalability
- Operator-run (not a broker)

---

### Tertiary ICP: Box Programme Customers

**Profile:**
- Companies with remote/hybrid employees
- Need employee-direct device returns
- Often triggered by:
  - Employee offboarding
  - Device refresh for remote workers
  - Office closure / WFH transition

**Key Requirement:** Simple, branded experience for employees

## 5. Outbound Strategy

### 5.1 Email Campaigns

**Campaign 1: Office Move Trigger**
- Target: Companies announcing office moves (LinkedIn, news)
- Timing: 1-3 months before move
- Message: "Moving offices? Don't forget your old IT equipment."

**Campaign 2: Device Refresh Cycle**
- Target: Companies with 3-4 year old devices (inferred from job postings, tech stack)
- Message: "Time to refresh your fleet? We handle the old devices."

**Campaign 3: Compliance Check-in**
- Target: Finance, healthcare, legal firms
- Message: "Is your device disposal GDPR-compliant?"

### 5.2 Cold Outreach Templates

**Template 1: Direct Approach**

> Subject: IT-aktiver hos [Company]?
>
> Hej [Name],
>
> Jeg hedder Kasper og driver Scandic IT — vi hjælper danske virksomheder med sikker afhentning og håndtering af udtjente computere, telefoner og servere.
>
> Har I en fast løsning for udtjente IT-aktiver? Mange virksomheder har en bunke gamle enheder i et hjørne — vi henter, sletter data certificeret, og I får både dokumentation og (ofte) lidt tilbage på værdierne.
>
> Må jeg ringe dig kort i næste uge?
>
> Mvh,  
> Kasper Horn  
> Scandic IT
