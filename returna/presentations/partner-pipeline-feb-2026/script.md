## Slide 2: What are Return Hubs? (40 sec)

Quick refresher on the model.

Return Hubs are ITAD contractors — companies that process IT equipment at end-of-life. They do the data erasure, diagnostics, refurbishment, and disposition.

Returna sits in the middle. We route enterprise device volume to these Return Hubs. They get steady work without doing sales. We get execution capacity without running warehouses.

What's in it for them? Four things: Enterprise volume routed directly to their facility. A free warehouse ERP module from us. Ninety percent of the execution fees. And thirty percent of resale proceeds.

That's the deal. Now let's look at who's in our pipeline.

## Slide 4: Ideal Partner Profile (50 sec)

Before I show you the prospects, it's worth understanding who we're looking for — and who we're avoiding.

Our sweet spot is smaller ITADs. Ten to fifty employees. ISO certified, especially 27001 for data security and 14001 for environmental. Revenue in the one to ten million euro range.

Critically — we want companies with no own asset management platform. That's our technology gap opportunity. They need us. We're not competing with their software.

What we avoid: Global ITAD networks like Ingram or Iron Mountain. They don't need our volume. Companies with their own platforms — they're competitors, not partners. And anyone with no certifications — our enterprise customers require it.

The goal is to find ITADs who are excellent at processing but struggle to get enterprise clients. That's where Returna adds value.
