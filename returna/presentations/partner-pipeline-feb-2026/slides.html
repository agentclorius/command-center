Ideal Return Hub Profile

We target smaller ITADs who need enterprise volume — not large networks who already have it.

What We Look For

✓ 10-50 employees (sweet spot)
✓ ISO 27001 + 14001 certified
✓ No own asset management platform
✓ Full ITAD processing capability
✓ Local/regional focus
✓ €1-10M revenue range

What We Avoid

✗ Global ITAD networks
✗ Companies with own platforms
✗ No certifications
✗ Revenue > €50M
