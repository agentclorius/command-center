# NIS2 Compliance & IT Asset Disposition
## How Returna Helps You Meet EU Cybersecurity Requirements

---

### The NIS2 Challenge

The **EU Network and Information Security Directive 2 (NIS2)** requires organizations to implement comprehensive cybersecurity measures — including **supply chain security** and **risk management** for all IT systems.

**But what happens when devices leave your organization?**

Every retired laptop, returned employee device, or decommissioned server is a potential compliance gap. Without proper IT Asset Disposition (ITAD), you risk:

- ❌ Data breaches from improperly wiped devices
- ❌ Supply chain vulnerabilities through unvetted disposal partners
- ❌ Audit failures due to missing documentation
- ❌ Regulatory penalties up to **€10M or 2% of global turnover**

---

### How Returna Addresses NIS2 Requirements

| NIS2 Requirement | How Returna Helps |
|------------------|-------------------|
| **Supply Chain Security** (Art. 21.2d) | Vetted Return Hub network with ISO 27001/14001 certifications. All partners meet standardized security requirements. |
| **Risk Management** (Art. 21.1) | Controlled, documented device retirement process. Full visibility from employee handover to final disposition. |
| **Data Security** (Art. 21.2) | Certified data erasure (NIST 800-88) with per-device certificates. Physical destruction available when required. |
| **Incident Handling** (Art. 21.2b) | Complete chain of custody. Full traceability if any device goes missing. Audit trail for every asset. |
| **Business Continuity** (Art. 21.2c) | Standardized processes across all regions. No single points of failure in your ITAD workflow. |
| **Audit & Compliance** (Art. 21.2g) | Per-device documentation ready for regulatory audits. Exportable compliance reports. |
