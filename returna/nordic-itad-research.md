# Nordic ITAD Research — Return Hub Prospects

_Research Date: 2026-02-05_
_Regions: Sweden, Norway, Finland, Iceland, Faroe Islands_
_Target: Small/medium ITADs with ISO certification, no competing platform_

---

## Executive Summary

**Key Finding:** The Nordic ITAD market is dominated by a few large players. Most small/medium companies are either:
1. Pure recyclers (no refurb/ITAD capability)
2. Subsidiaries of larger groups
3. Municipal waste operations

**Recommended Approach:**
- Use certification directories (ADISA, R2, ISO) to find certified smaller players
- Search LinkedIn for local ITAD operations managers
- Check local business registries for company size data

## Certification Directory Findings (2026-02-05)

### ADISA Certified Companies — Nordic Result: ZERO

Checked ADISA full directory (adisacertification.com). **No Nordic companies found.**

ADISA members are predominantly:
- United Kingdom (majority)
- Germany (Vyta, Procurri)
- Italy, Greece, Poland, Ireland, Japan, USA

**Implication:** Nordic ITADs don't pursue ADISA certification. They likely rely on:
- ISO 27001 (data security)
- ISO 14001 (environmental)
- National WEEE compliance

### R2 Certified Companies — Nordic

R2 (SERI) has searchable database but requires interactive search.
Known European R2: Huiskes (Netherlands), Flex IT (Netherlands — "first Dutch R2")

**Action needed:** Manual search of SERI database for Nordic facilities

## Key Insight

The large players (Foxway, Inrego, 3stepIT, Stena, Revac) dominate the Nordic market. Finding small/medium ITADs with proper certifications will require:

1. **Going local** — city-level searches for smaller operators
2. **Checking certification databases** — ISO/ADISA members who aren't on the big lists
3. **LinkedIn outreach** — find individuals at smaller operations
4. **MSP/IT service companies** — some do ITAD as secondary service
