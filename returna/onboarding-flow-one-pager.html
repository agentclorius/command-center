📋 Survey Collects
  • Company info & contact details
  • Countries served & depot locations
  • ISO 27001 / ISO 14001 / WEEE status
  • Data erasure tools & methods
  • ITAD capabilities (intake → disposition)
  • Repair & refurb level (TRC-aligned)
  • Logistics & box program readiness
  • Monthly processing capacity
  • Resale channels & downstream control
  • Motivation for joining Returna
⚡ Scoring Engine
  • Certifications — ISO 27001, 14001, WEEE weighted highest
  • Capabilities — erasure, diagnostics, refurb scored per level
  • Capacity — monthly throughput (devices/month)
  • Geography — coverage in priority regions scores higher
  • Experience — years active, enterprise references
  • Tech readiness — API/integration capability
  • Score maps to suggested tier (Cat 1/2/3)
  • Below minimum threshold → flagged for review
🚀 Once Active
  • Tier determines eligible project types
  • Volume routing based on geography + tier
  • Performance KPIs monitored from day 1
  • SLA breaches trigger throttle/reroute
  • Quarterly review cycles
  • Tier upgrades via TRC training + re-assessment
  • Full ERP access (warehouse module)
  • 90% execution fee + 30% resale proceeds

✅ Non-Negotiable Requirements (All Tiers)

  • ISO 27001 for data-bearing assets
  • Certified data erasure tooling
  • ISO 14001 + WEEE for recycling
  • Platform-enforced evidence & chain-of-custody
  • Erasure before any other outcome
  • Accept Returna's tier definitions
  • Accept performance-based routing
