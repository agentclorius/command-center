## Post Option B (Trend-focused)

3 forces reshaping circular tech in 2026:

**1. Device prices keep climbing**
€1,000+ smartphones. Expensive laptops. Trade-in value matters more than ever.

**2. Upgrade cycles are lengthening**
People hold devices longer. Trade-in creates upgrade urgency without discounting.

**3. Regulation is tightening**
NIS2. CSRD reporting. Right to Repair. Companies with circular practices gain real advantage.

The result? Trade-in is moving from cost center to value driver.

The mobile industry proved it works. Now enterprise IT is next.
