# CCS Insight Circular Markets Event — February 2026

_Source: Industry event recap (shared 2026-02-21)_

## Key Themes

### 1. Trade-in Evolving into Strategic Lever
- Shifting from sustainability initiative to competitive strategy
- Especially for carriers: "guaranteed" and "future" value offers as differentiators
- **Gap:** 40%+ take-back in USA vs 10-20% in parts of Europe

### 2. Smartphones as Gateway
- Mobile leads adoption, normalises trade-in and renewed purchasing
- Creates proven blueprint to extend circularity beyond mobile into wider consumer portfolios

### 3. Consumer Behaviour is Essential
- Success starts with understanding how consumers think about value, timing and access
- Refurbished/renewed products aren't about cannibalisation
- Opens new segments: access to premium devices, earlier upgrades, engaging consumers outside upgrade cycle

### 4. Scale Demands Simplicity
- Execution becomes the key differentiator as programmes grow
- Requirements:
  - Seamless consumer journeys
  - Automation
  - Consistency
  - Predictable outcomes
- Essential for driving participation and sustainable supply

### 5. Renewed Going Mainstream
- Pre-owned/refurbished increasingly viewed as credible purchase option
- Particularly strong among younger demographics
- Trust guarantees and quality standards shifting perception
- Awareness still varies across markets and categories

## Key Insight

> "The opportunity isn't about whether circularity works, it's about how well it's designed and delivered."
