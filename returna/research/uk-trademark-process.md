## Why UK Registration is Needed

- EU trademark (EUIPO) does NOT cover UK since Brexit (Jan 2021)
- Returna has 10 UK pipeline prospects (Vyta, S2S Group, Tier1, Secure ITAD, etc.)
- UK registration protects the brand for UK Return Hubs and UK enterprise customers

## Classes to Register

Same as EU application:
- **Class 9:** Downloadable computer software
- **Class 42:** Platform as a Service (PaaS)

## ⚠️ Key Requirement: UK Address for Service

Since Brexit (Jan 2021), **non-UK applicants must appoint a UK-based Address for Service (AFS)**.

This means Returna ApS (Danish company) cannot file directly without one of:
1. **UK trademark attorney** (recommended) — handles filing + acts as AFS
2. **UK registered agent service** — provides address only
3. **Madrid Protocol via WIPO** — alternative route, no UK AFS required for initial filing
