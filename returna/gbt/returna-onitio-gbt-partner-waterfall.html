Return Hubs

  • Parts, depot, logistics, and recovery
  • No default passive revenue share

Payout philosophy

Returna retains

  • Contract risk and pricing logic
  • Scope management and approvals
  • Governance and orchestration margin

Onitio gets paid for

  • Field-service labor
  • Completed onsite event
  • Approved service activity

Hubs get paid for

  • Part supplied
  • Handling and fulfilment
  • Depot work
  • Recovery and logistics

Best working model: Returna owns the enterprise relationship and routes work by incident type. Onitio is paid per field-service event. Return Hubs are paid only when they contribute operational value. This preserves clean enterprise ownership while still giving hubs visible economics.
