Low scenario

  • €90 per device / year
  • Only sensible with narrow scope
  • Works best for young, stable fleets
  • Very limited downside protection

Base scenario

  • €100-110 per device / year
  • Best practical commercial range
  • Strongest starting point for discussion
  • Needs controlled claim scope

High scenario

  • €120-130 per device / year
  • Better for mixed or older fleets
  • Supports broader service obligations
  • More room for risk and governance
